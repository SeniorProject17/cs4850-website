Welcome to our Senior Project Website for the SP17 Blue Chrome Plugin

The purpose of this Spotify Chrome plug-in is to enhance the user experience for Spotify listeners. Our goal is to allow users to skip, like, and shuffle songs directly from their Chrome browser window without needing to open the Spotify app
